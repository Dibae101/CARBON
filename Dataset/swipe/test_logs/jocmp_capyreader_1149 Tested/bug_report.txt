### Steps to Reproduce

If I want to use the zoom gesture (pull two fingers apart) within an article, e.g. to take a closer look at a picture, this does not work properly as other gestures seem to be preferred.
I can only zoom in in very small steps, but often other gestures are recognized, e.g. “next article” as when swiping to the left.
When I finally succeed in zooming in, I can only scroll down and up in the article, but not to the left and right.

### Expected behavior

Zoom gesture is recognized correctly and I can zoom in and out continuously without interruption.
If the article has been zoomed, the gestures should be deactivated to navigate in the article.
